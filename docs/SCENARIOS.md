## Beginner Scenarios

Perfect for learning the basics.

### Basic Single Approach
**Difficulty**: â­ Beginner

**Scenario Details**:
- **Aircraft Count**: 1 aircraft
- **Weather**: Calm winds (0-5 knots)
- **Visibility**: 10+ nautical miles (unlimited)
- **Runway**: Single active runway
- **Complexity**: Simple descent and landing

**Objectives**:
1. Monitor the incoming aircraft
2. Issue descent clearance to 2,500 feet
3. Issue descent clearance to 1,500 feet
4. Align aircraft with runway
5. Clear for landing

**Key Skills**:
- Reading radar displays
- Using descent commands
- Heading commands for alignment
- Landing clearances

**Expected Time**: 3-5 minutes

**Success Criteria**:
- Aircraft lands safely
- No altitude busts (descending through restricted altitudes)
- No separation violations
- Minimum 2 clearances to land

### Two-Aircraft Sequential
**Difficulty**: â­ Beginner

**Scenario Details**:
- **Aircraft Count**: 2 aircraft arriving sequentially (5 minutes apart)
- **Weather**: Light winds (5-10 knots)
- **Visibility**: 8+ nautical miles
- **Separation**: Standard minimums
- **Complexity**: Simple sequencing

**Objectives**:
1. Land first aircraft
2. Reset systems (30 seconds)
3. Land second aircraft

**Key Skills**:
- Sequencing operations
- Radio discipline
- Clearance timing

**Expected Time**: 12-15 minutes

**Success Criteria**:
- Both aircraft land safely
- Proper separation maintained
- No go-arounds required

### VFR Flight Following
**Difficulty**: â­ Beginner

**Scenario Details**:
- **Aircraft Count**: 1 VFR (Visual Flight Rules) aircraft
- **Aircraft Type**: Cessna 172 or similar
- **Speed**: ~100 knots
- **Altitude**: 3,000 feet
- **Pattern**: Standard traffic pattern

**Objectives**:
1. Provide flight following service
2. Guide through traffic pattern
3. Clear for landing

**Key Skills**:
- VFR procedures
- Traffic pattern navigation
- Lower speed handling

**Expected Time**: 8-10 minutes

**Success Criteria**:
- Successful landing
- Flight following maintained
- Proper pattern execution

## Achievements

Complete achievements by mastering scenarios:

- **Newcomer**: Complete any Beginner scenario
- **Rookie**: Complete 5 Beginner scenarios
- **Professional**: Complete 3 Intermediate scenarios
- **Veteran**: Complete 5 Advanced scenarios
- **Master**: Complete all Expert scenarios
- **Legend**: Score > 90 on 10 Expert scenarios

## Performance Metrics

**Efficiency Rating**:
- Excellent: > 80%
- Good: 70-80%
- Average: 60-70%
- Poor: < 60%

**Safety Rating**:
- Excellent: 0 violations
- Good: 1-2 minor violations
- Average: 3-4 violations
- Poor: 5+ violations
